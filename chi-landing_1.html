Built-in protection

Safety is not small print.

Every feature in CHI is built around the understanding that natural does not mean safe to combine freely. The safety architecture is visible, central, and always on.

⚠

Health Profile Filtering

Build your profile with medications, conditions, and sensitivities. Every search result, builder formula, and AI response is automatically cross-referenced — hard rejections for known clashes, advisory flags for moderate interactions.

⚗

Interaction Checker

Full herb-pharma and herb-herb interaction database. Covers the CYP450 enzyme pathway in plain language. Real examples: St John's Wort and antidepressants, ginkgo and blood thinners. Food-drug interactions included.

✦

Honest Language Framework

Platform-wide compliance with UK MHRA guidelines. No "treats," "cures," or "heals" anywhere on CHI. Not because we're required to — because that's what intellectual honesty looks like applied to health information.
