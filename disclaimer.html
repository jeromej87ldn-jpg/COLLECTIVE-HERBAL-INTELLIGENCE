Medical Disclaimer

Please read this disclaimer carefully before using any part of the C.H.I platform. It applies to all tools, content, and community posts on CHI.

CHI is not a medical service

C.H.I (Collective Herbal Intelligence) is an educational platform providing herbal knowledge and information. Nothing on this platform — including the Herb Match, Herbadex, Herb Identifier, community posts, or any other content — constitutes medical advice, medical diagnosis, or medical treatment.

Using CHI does not create a patient-practitioner relationship of any kind. CHI is not a substitute for professional medical care.

What CHI provides

CHI provides educational information about herbs, plants, and herbal traditions from around the world. All herb-related content on CHI is presented for educational and informational purposes only.

CHI uses language consistent with UK MHRA guidelines throughout:

  • We say "traditionally used for" — not "treats" or "cures"
  • We say "may support" — not "will heal" or "eliminates"
  • We say "research suggests" — not "proven to treat"

This language reflects the honest state of herbal knowledge — not a legal technicality.

The Herb Match

The Herb Match generates personalised herb information based on the health profile you provide. It is an educational tool — not a diagnostic instrument.

The Herb Match does not diagnose medical conditions. It does not prescribe treatment. It does not replace a consultation with a qualified healthcare professional.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting any herbal protocol, particularly if you:

  • Are taking prescription medications
  • Have a diagnosed medical condition
  • Are pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Are treating a child under 18
  • Have serious or worsening symptoms

The Herb Identifier

The Herb Identifier analyses photographs to suggest possible plant identifications. It is an educational tool — not a reliable foraging guide.

Never consume or handle any wild plant based solely on the Herb Identifier's output. Plant misidentification can cause serious harm or death. Always verify plant identification through multiple expert sources — field guides, qualified botanists, or mycologists — before consuming or handling any wild plant.

CHI accepts no liability for any harm arising from plant misidentification.

Medication interactions

Some herbs interact with pharmaceutical medications. CHI's Herb Match includes a medication interaction flag but this is not exhaustive. It is a starting point — not a guarantee of safety.

Always inform your doctor or pharmacist of any herbs you are taking or considering taking alongside prescription medications.

Community content

Community posts on CHI represent personal experiences and opinions of individual members. They have not been reviewed by healthcare professionals. They do not constitute medical advice.

CHI encourages members to share experiences using honest, non-prescriptive language — but cannot guarantee the accuracy of all community contributions.

Emergency situations

If you or someone else is experiencing a medical emergency, call 999 (UK) or your local emergency services immediately. Do not use CHI in place of emergency medical care.

In summary: CHI is a place to learn about herbs — their history, their traditional uses, and the research around them. It is not a replacement for qualified medical care. Use CHI to grow your knowledge. Use your healthcare provider to make decisions about your health.
