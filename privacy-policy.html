Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025 · C.H.I — Collective Herbal Intelligence

We have written this policy in plain English because you deserve to understand exactly what happens with your data — not just that a legal requirement has been met. CHI handles sensitive health information. We take that seriously.

The short versionWe collect only what we need. We never sell your data. We never share it with advertisers. We never use it for any purpose other than giving you the best herbal knowledge platform possible. Your health information is yours.

2. What data we collect

Account information

When you create a CHI account, we collect your email address, an encrypted password, and any display name or username you choose to add.

Health information — special category data

ImportantHealth data is classified as special category data under UK GDPR and receives the highest level of legal protection. We process this data only with your explicit consent and only to provide personalised herb recommendations.

When you use the Herb Match, you may choose to share your gender, age, life stage, medications, health conditions, and symptoms. Every piece of this information is voluntary.

Platform usage data

  • Herbs searched, saved, or added to your Herb Passport
  • Journal entries you write
  • Herb Match query history
  • Community posts, comments, and messages
  • Your Herb Passport status for each herb

What we store in your browser

CHI stores your colour theme preference, knowledge level, and — if you add one — your Anthropic API key in your browser's localStorage. This data is stored on your device only and is never transmitted to CHI servers.

3. Why we collect your data

The primary reason is to run the platform and give you access to the tools you have paid for. For health data specifically, we rely on your explicit consent — the strongest legal basis available under UK GDPR.

4. How your data interacts with AI

Plain EnglishHerb profiles and Herb Match results are generated using Claude, an AI by Anthropic. When you make a query, relevant information from your profile is sent to Anthropic's API servers to generate your response.

You could ask any AI assistant a question about herbs. What CHI gives you is different — your health profile, your herb history, your journal, and your preferences all informing every answer. The AI is the engine. CHI is the platform built specifically for your herbal journey.

Anthropic does not use your query data to train their AI models when accessed via the API. Your uploaded photographs for the Herb Identifier are used solely to identify the plant shown and are not stored beyond your session.

5. How we protect your data

No social login — by design

CHI does not offer Google, Facebook, or Apple login. Your health information has no business being linked to third-party platform accounts. This is a principle, not a technical limitation.

No advertising. Ever.

CHI does not display advertising and does not work with advertising networks. Your data is never used to build advertising profiles.

No data selling. Ever.

CHI has never sold personal data and will never sell personal data. Not to data brokers. Not to supplement companies. Not to anyone.

6. Who we share your data with

We share data with as few parties as possible. The complete list: Anthropic (AI query processing), Supabase (data storage), Stripe (payment processing), Netlify (platform hosting), and affiliate partners (only when you click a purchase link). We share data with nobody else.

7. How long we keep your data

We keep your data for as long as you have an active account. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Financial transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by HMRC.

8. Your rights under UK GDPR

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — have inaccurate data corrected
  • Deletion — request permanent deletion of all your data
  • Portability — export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent — stop us processing your health data at any time
  • Complain — raise a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk

9. Cookies and localStorage

CHI uses session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. We use your browser's localStorage for preferences — this data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us.

10. Children

CHI is for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe your child has created an account, contact us immediately at [privacy@chi.com].
